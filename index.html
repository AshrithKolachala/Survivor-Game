How to Play

Move with WASD or the on-screen joystick (mobile). Your character auto-fires at the nearest target — focus on surviving and dodging. Kill enemies to collect XP orbs, level up, and choose upgrades. Bosses spawn every 5 waves and drop massive XP on death.
